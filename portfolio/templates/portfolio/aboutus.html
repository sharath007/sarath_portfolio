About FarmConnect

Farm Connect is an organisation seeking to help farmers and the people related to Farming sector. This is an effective alternative to the in-efficient supply chain that exists today.
We, Farm connect act as a medium to connect farmers and the related businessmen. Our mission is to create a better connection all over the Agri business by bringing farmers, retailers, Agricultural labours, Agricultural Engineers and farming related businessmen under one roof, thus decreasing the need of middlemen.


Good for farmers, Good for the economy, Good for the earth.
